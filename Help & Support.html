Help & Support

Find answers to common questions, troubleshooting tips, and contact options.

Account Management

How do I reset my password?

If you forgot your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How can I update my account information?

To update your account details, go to your profile settings page. You can edit your name, email, and other personal information there.

How do I cancel my subscription?

If you'd like to cancel your subscription, please visit the 'Subscription' section in your account settings and follow the instructions to cancel.

Billing

How do I update my payment method?

To update your payment method, go to the 'Billing' section in your account settings and follow the instructions to add or update your payment details.

Why was my payment declined?

There could be several reasons for a declined payment. Please check with your bank or payment provider for more details.

Device Support

What devices are supported?

Our service is available on a wide range of devices including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, and web browsers.

How do I install the app on my device?

Visit the app store on your device and search for 'Stream'. Follow the instructions to download and install the app.

Streaming Issues

Why is my video buffering?

Buffering can occur due to a slow internet connection. Try restarting your router or checking your internet speed.

How do I improve video quality?

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and adjust the video quality settings in the app.

Troubleshooting

If you're facing issues with streaming, try the following steps:

  • Check your internet connection to ensure it's stable.
  • Try restarting the app or refreshing the page.
  • If the issue persists, clear your browser's cache and cookies.
  • Make sure your device's operating system and app are up to date.
